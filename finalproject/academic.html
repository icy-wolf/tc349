Michigan State University

This is my second year here at MSU. I participate in many groups around campus such as Women in Computing, Women in Engineering and Mixed Martial Arts. I am a community relations member on the WIC E-Board and WIE mentor to freshmen girls in computer science. MMA is something unique about me and is always fun to do between classes.

Lakeview High School

Lakeview was my home high school as I went to two different high schools in my high school career. I was part of National Honors Society and was one of twenty-six to graduate with 4.0. Through NHS I would volunteer at our local zoo for the Zoo Boo and be a part of the student run Read-to-Lead program where I would encourage 3rd-5th graders to read.

Battle Creek Area Math and Science Center

The BCAMSC was the second high school I attended. This is where I took my core math, science, engineering and technology courses. I was part of the VEX robotics team as a builder and driver. We attended two competitions during my time there. I was also on the student advisory board and would help plan our annual lock-in.
